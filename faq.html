FAQ:


  • Which image formats can I upload to PicDrop?


    Without Transcoding (Auto-Convert): .PNG, .JP(E)G, .GIF (either animated or not)
    With Transcoding: Wayyyy to many. Have a quick look at this table.

  • Am I allowed to integrate PicDrop into my application? If yes, how?


    For sure! Just POST your HTML File Object Data to http://picdrop.com/upload.php in the field "file".
    It will return the ID of the image.
    The Image will be available under these URLS:
    • http://picdrop.tk/myID1 (HTML Preview Page)
    • http://picdrop.tk/myID1/raw (Will return a 302 Header to the location of the Image in our CDN)

  • Can I auto-upload Images from ShareX to PicDrop?


    Yup! But it's dangerous to go alone, take this http://picdrop.tk/KnH58 and this http://picdrop.tk/sharex.json
